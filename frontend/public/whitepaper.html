🔷 Introduction

What is PARAS REWARD?

PARAS REWARD is a revolutionary rewards platform where users earn PRC (Paras Reward Coins) through daily mining activities. These coins can be redeemed for real-world benefits.

🎯 Vision: "Reward Points → Real Utility"

Unlike other reward apps where points expire or have limited use, every PRC you earn has real utility from Day 1.

The Problem:

Millions of Indians earn reward points but can never USE them. Points expire, have limited options, or require huge minimums.

Our Solution:

Complete Earn → Use → Redeem ecosystem. Pay bills, recharge mobiles, buy vouchers, or transfer to bank!
