- **Profit Wallets (Master/Sub/Outlet):** Hold delivery shares and deposit returns.
- **Stock Movement:** Company → Master → Sub → Outlet → Customer flow, tracked with QR/batch no.
- **Security Deposit:** Master/Sub/Outlet provide deposits, receiving 3% monthly return. Annual renewal fees.
