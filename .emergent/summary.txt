<product_requirements>
The PARAS REWARD application is a crypto-mining platform (10 PRC = ₹1 INR) with multi-item cart, atomic PRC deduction, and KYC-dependent cashback. It features multi-wallet financial systems, RBAC dashboards, password recovery, and support tickets. Enhancements include real-time notifications, analytics, pagination, offline PWA, advanced search/filters, referral/gamification dashboards, flash sales, and a PRC Treasure Hunt game.

Key additions:
-   **Free User Plan**: Mining with 2-day PRC validity, Treasure Hunt PRC usage, minimum ₹1000 cashback withdrawal, dynamic cashback.
-   **Flexible KYC Document Selection**: Aadhaar or PAN submission.
-   **Enhanced Notification System**: Large, animated notifications.
-   **Manager Role Dashboard**: Comprehensive management (users, orders, reports, products, finance, communication, support, stockists).
-   **Image Upload Enhancements**: Client-side crop, resize, compress for KYC/product images.
-   **Admin Dashboard Redesign**: Modern, unified dashboard with quick access.
-   **Role-Based Redirection**: Correct login/logout for all roles.
-   **Treasure Hunt PRC Check**: Proactive low balance check with MINE NOW.
-   **Multi-language Support**: Hindi, English, Marathi, Gujarati.
-   **Android App Download**: Direct APK download from homepage.
-   **Admin Social Media Settings**: Configure social media links for display on homepage/footer.
-   **Animated Logo**: Visual enhancement for brand logo.
-   **Scratch Card Game**: Cashback for VIP (10-50%) and Free (0-10%) users based on 10, 50, 100 PRC cards.
-   **PRC Analytics Dashboard**: Graphical display of total PRC mined/consumed for admin.
</product_requirements>
